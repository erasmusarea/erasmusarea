Summary

The project aims to provide high-quality education opportunities for vocational students and teachers by integrating digital, green and key competences into the learning process. Through international mobility, internships and job shadowing activities, participants will experience real work environments, innovative teaching methods and European cooperation.

15 students will participate in a structured internship programme in partner institutions abroad, while 6 teachers will take part in job shadowing activities to observe and implement innovative, student-centered teaching approaches in their own school.

Planned Activities

The project is structured into several key activities designed to support learning, teaching and training.

Preparation Phase

Selection of participants, language preparation, cultural orientation, and technical training on digital tools.

Sustainability Plan

Ensuring that the project’s impact continues beyond its official end date.

  • Integrating digital and sustainable practices into the school curriculum.
  • Continuing cooperation with partner organisations for future projects.
  • Using project outputs in regular lessons and trainings.
